Calculator Methodology

Our Calculations Are Based On:

  • Industry Research: Studies show employees waste 5-12 minutes per meeting on AV/IT issues (Harvard Business Review, Barco ClickShare studies)
  • Billable vs Non-Billable Time: We assume 50% of wasted time represents lost billable hours; the other 50% is productivity loss valued at support staff rates
  • Modern AV Performance: 85% reduction in technical issues based on our 22 years of installation data and client feedback

  • System Lifespan: Professional AV systems typically last 7-10 years; we use a conservative 5-year calculation period
