## ğŸ¯ Deployment Steps (Use Publish Tab)

### **Step 1: Locate the Publish Tab**
Look for the **"Publish"** or **"Deploy"** button/tab in this interface (usually at the top right or in a menu).

### **Step 2: Choose Deployment Method**

The Publish tab typically offers these options:

#### **Option A: Direct Deploy (Easiest)**
- Click **"Deploy to Production"** or **"Publish Now"**
- The system will automatically deploy your site
- You'll get a live URL instantly
- All 25 logos will work immediately

#### **Option B: Download & Deploy to Netlify**
1. Click **"Download Project"** or **"Export Files"**
2. Save the ZIP file to your computer
3. Go to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
4. Drag and drop your ZIP file
5. Wait 30 seconds - site is live!
6. You'll get a URL like: `random-name-12345.netlify.app`

#### **Option C: Deploy to Vercel**
1. Download project files
2. Go to [Vercel](https://vercel.com)
3. Click "Add New Project"
4. Upload your files
5. Click "Deploy"
