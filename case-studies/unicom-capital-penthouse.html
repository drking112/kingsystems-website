Premium AV Integration for Top-Floor Financial Services Headquarters

Project Overview

When Unicom Capital moved to the prestigious top floor of 1144 Fifteenth in downtown Denver, they required a comprehensive AV technology solution befitting their position as a leading financial services firm. King Systems delivered a complete integration featuring executive-grade displays, trading floor video walls, and enterprise-class audiovisual systems throughout their premium 17,500 square foot penthouse space.

The project showcased cutting-edge technology in a stunning architectural setting with 360-degree mountain and city views. Our team designed and installed systems for the executive reception area, trading floor with real-time financial displays, conference rooms, and private offices—all engineered to support mission-critical financial operations.
