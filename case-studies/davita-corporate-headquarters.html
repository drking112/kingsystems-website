Enterprise AV Technology for Healthcare Industry Leader

Project Overview

King Systems partnered with DaVita, one of the nation's leading healthcare providers, to deliver comprehensive AV technology solutions for their corporate headquarters. As a Fortune 500 company managing kidney care facilities nationwide, DaVita required enterprise-grade systems that support executive decision-making, training initiatives, and seamless collaboration across their organization.

Our team designed and installed integrated AV systems throughout their Denver headquarters, including executive conference rooms, training facilities, and collaborative workspaces—all engineered to support DaVita's mission-critical healthcare operations.
