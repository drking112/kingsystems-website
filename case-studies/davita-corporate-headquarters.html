Portfolio DaVita Corporate Headquarters

Project Overview

King Systems partnered with DaVita, one of the nation's leading providers of kidney care services, to design and implement comprehensive audiovisual technology solutions for their corporate headquarters. This enterprise-scale project required sophisticated AV systems that support executive leadership, enable seamless collaboration across distributed teams, and facilitate critical healthcare business operations.

Our scope included state-of-the-art conference room technology, executive boardrooms with high-end video conferencing capabilities, and modern collaboration spaces equipped for hybrid work environments. The installation supports DaVita's mission-critical communications while maintaining the professional standards expected in a Fortune 500 healthcare organization.

Client: DaVita is a leading provider of dialysis services with headquarters in Denver, serving over 200,000 patients at nearly 3,000 centers across the United States.

Challenges

  • Enterprise Scale: Supporting hundreds of employees across multiple floors with consistent AV experiences

  • Hybrid Work: Seamless integration between on-site and remote participants in meetings

Solutions

  • Secure Architecture: Enterprise-grade network segmentation and encrypted video conferencing
  • Phased Installation: Evening and weekend work to avoid disrupting critical business functions
  • Zoom Rooms: Fully integrated Zoom Rooms with intelligent camera tracking and noise cancellation
