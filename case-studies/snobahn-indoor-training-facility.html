Portfolio SnoBahn Indoor Training Facility

Project Overview

King Systems designed and installed a comprehensive audiovisual entertainment system for SnoBahn, Colorado's premier indoor ski and snowboard training facility. This unique venue combines athletic training with social entertainment spaces, requiring integrated AV solutions that enhance both the athletic experience and customer hospitality.

The installation features a massive 217-inch diagonal LED video wall on the spring floor training area, creating an immersive visual experience for athletes and spectators. Throughout the facility, we deployed enterprise-grade displays, distributed audio systems, and centralized video distribution—all controlled via intuitive iPad interfaces.

From the bar and party rooms to the BMX area and breakroom, every space features custom-tailored AV technology that supports SnoBahn's unique blend of serious training and social entertainment. The system includes Sonos streaming audio, Q-SYS processing, AVPro Edge video distribution, and professional MaxHub displays throughout.
