Project Overview

King Systems was selected to provide comprehensive AV integration for the Denver Art Museum's stunning Lanny & Sharon Martin Building. This architectural masterpiece required cutting-edge technology solutions that would enhance the visitor experience while complementing the building's iconic design.

Our team delivered digital signage systems, distributed audio, interactive displays, and event space AV throughout multiple galleries and public spaces. The project showcases our ability to work with world-class institutions on complex, high-profile installations.
