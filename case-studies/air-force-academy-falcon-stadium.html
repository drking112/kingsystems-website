Project Overview

King Systems partnered with the US Air Force Academy to deliver comprehensive AV technology upgrades across multiple premium areas of Falcon Stadium, home to 46,692 passionate fans and one of college football's most iconic venues. This high-profile government project required secure installation practices, strict compliance protocols, and enterprise-grade systems designed to enhance both the athlete experience and premium hospitality spaces.

Our scope included state-of-the-art audiovisual systems for the home team locker room, the prestigious Blue and Silver Club facility, and luxury suites—creating an elevated experience for players, recruits, donors, and VIP guests. The project was executed in phases to minimize disruption during the football season, with installations coordinated around team schedules and game days.

Scope of Work

Home Team Locker Room

  • Large format displays with Crestron NVX video distribution for game strategy and motivational content
  • Q-SYS professional audio systems for team addresses and presentations
  • Video playback systems for game footage review and analysis
  • Crestron integrated control systems for one-touch operation

Blue & Silver Club

  • Premium hospitality displays with Crestron NVX 4K video distribution and digital signage
  • Q-SYS distributed audio throughout club spaces with zone control
  • Multi-zone video distribution via Crestron NVX network infrastructure
  • High-definition broadcast integration with flexible source routing

Luxury Suites

  • 4K displays in each luxury suite with Crestron NVX video distribution
  • Q-SYS premium audio systems with independent zone control
