Project Overview

King Systems partnered with the US Air Force Academy to deliver comprehensive AV technology upgrades across multiple premium areas of Falcon Stadium. This high-profile government project required secure installation practices, strict compliance protocols, and systems designed to enhance both the athlete experience and premium hospitality spaces.

Our scope included state-of-the-art audiovisual systems for the home team locker room, the prestigious Blue and Silver Club facility, and luxury suites—creating an elevated experience for players, recruits, donors, and VIP guests.
