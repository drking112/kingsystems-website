Project Overview

For over 15 years, King Systems has served as a trusted technology partner to Comcast, one of the world's largest telecommunications and media conglomerates. This ongoing enterprise engagement represents one of the largest and most sophisticated commercial AV deployments we've ever undertaken—spanning more than 800 conference rooms, 180 training facilities, and three state-of-the-art Executive Briefing Centers across the United States.

Our nationwide scope of work encompasses the complete lifecycle of audiovisual technology: design, engineering, installation, integration, training, and ongoing support. From standardized huddle rooms to mission-critical executive presentation spaces, King Systems delivers consistent, reliable AV experiences that enable Comcast's business operations and strategic communications.

Beyond conference and training rooms, our partnership includes digital signage networks, cafeteria menu boards, enterprise sound masking systems, and specialized collaboration technology—demonstrating the breadth and depth of our capabilities in supporting a Fortune 50 enterprise client.
