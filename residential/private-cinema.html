Private Cinema Design & Installation

Cinematic experiences in the comfort of your Colorado home

Transform your basement, bonus room, or dedicated space into a world-class private cinema. King Systems designs and installs custom private cinemas throughout Colorado, from Crested Butte to Denver, featuring premium acoustics, cutting-edge projection, and immersive audio.

Whether you're a movie enthusiast, sports fan, or gaming aficionado, we create spaces that rival commercial cinemas—with the comfort and convenience of home.

4K/8K Projection

Sony, JVC, and Epson projectors with native 4K resolution, HDR support, and laser light sources for stunning image quality on screens up to 200 inches.

Dolby Atmos Audio

Immersive 3D sound with height channels, premium speakers from McIntosh, Bowers & Wilkins, or James Loudspeaker, and acoustically treated rooms.

Luxury Seating

Premium theater seating with power recliners, heated seats, built-in tactile transducers, and custom upholstery to match your home's aesthetic.

Smart Control

One-touch control via Crestron or Control4—dim lights, close shades, start your movie, and adjust volume from a single remote or mobile app.

Acoustic Treatment

Professional room analysis, bass traps, acoustic panels, and soundproofing to optimize audio performance and prevent sound bleed to adjacent rooms.

Gaming Integration

Low-latency 4K gaming with PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and PC support, plus custom lighting effects that sync with on-screen action.
