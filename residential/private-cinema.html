Theater Configurations for Every Space

Intimate Screening Room

Perfect for couples or small families—4 to 8 seats with high-end audio and immersive projection.

  • • 100"-150" screen with 4K/HDR projection
  • • 5.1 or 7.1 surround sound
  • • Sonus Faber or bookshelf speakers
  • • Compact room acoustics

Family Theater

The heart of home entertainment—8 to 12 seats with Dolby Atmos and game console integration.

  • • 120"-180" screen with laser projection
  • • Dolby Atmos 7.1.4 or 9.1.4 system
  • • Pro Audio Technology in-wall/ceiling speakers
  • • Gaming and streaming optimization

Grand Cinema

Your private movie palace—12 to 20+ seats with reference-grade performance and luxury finishes.

  • • 150"-250" screen with high-lumen projection
  • • Dolby Atmos 15+ channel system
  • • Pro Audio Technology reference speakers
  • • Tiered seating with riser platforms

Multi-Purpose Media Room

Theater, gaming, and entertaining—flexible spaces that adapt to how you live.

  • • Retractable screen or large-format TV
  • • 5.1 or 7.1 system with in-ceiling speakers
  • • Lighting scenes for movies and socializing
  • • Flexible furniture arrangements
