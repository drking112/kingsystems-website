Lighting That Enhances Your Wellbeing

Colorado's dramatic mountain light deserves lighting control that rises to the occasion. King Systems specializes in circadian lighting systems from Ketra, dmf Lighting, and Lutron—technologies that automatically adjust color temperature and intensity throughout the day to support your natural rhythms, enhance alertness when you need it, and promote restful sleep at night.

More than just dimming, our systems create dynamic lighting environments that adapt to how you live—from energizing morning light in your kitchen to warm, calming tones in your bedroom at sunset.

Ketra: The Future of Light

Ketra represents the pinnacle of lighting technology—a system that doesn't just dim, but dynamically adjusts color temperature and spectrum to match natural sunlight. As the sun moves across the Colorado sky, Ketra seamlessly shifts from energizing cool tones in the morning to warm, relaxing hues in the evening.

  • Vibrancy Tuning™ for perfect color rendering
  • NatureConnect mode mimics natural daylight patterns
