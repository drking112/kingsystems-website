Lighting Control & Automation

Intelligent lighting and shade systems for Colorado luxury homes

Transform your home with intelligent lighting control from Lutron, the world leader in smart lighting. King Systems designs and installs sophisticated lighting systems that enhance comfort, save energy, and adapt to Colorado's dramatic mountain light throughout the day.

From automated shades that protect fine art from UV damage to circadian lighting that supports your natural sleep cycle, we create lighting environments that are beautiful, functional, and effortless to control.

Scene Control

One-touch scenes for every occasion—"Movie Night" dims lights, closes shades. "Good Morning" gradually raises lights and opens shades.

Automated Shades

Lutron motorized shades for panoramic windows, skylights, and difficult-to-reach locations. Protect furnishings from UV damage automatically.

Circadian Lighting

Tunable white lighting that mimics natural daylight progression, supporting your body's natural rhythms and improving sleep quality.

Energy Efficiency

Occupancy sensors, daylight harvesting, and LED dimming reduce energy consumption by up to 60% while maintaining perfect lighting.

Smart Control

Control from wall keypads, mobile apps, voice assistants, or integrate with Crestron, Savant, and Control4 smart home systems.

Custom Finishes

Designer keypads and dimmers in hundreds of colors and finishes to match your home's aesthetic—from brushed nickel to custom wood.

Colorado-Specific Lighting Considerations

  • High-Altitude UV Protection: Automated shades protect artwork, furniture, and flooring from Colorado's intense UV exposure.
  • Snow Glare Management: Shades and lighting scenes compensate for harsh winter snow reflection in mountain homes.
  • Astronomical Timeclock: Automatically adjusts for sunrise/sunset times that vary dramatically across seasons.
  • Outdoor Lighting: Dusk-to-dawn pathway lighting, deck lighting, and landscape accents that withstand mountain weather.
