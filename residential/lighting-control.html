Yes! Smart lighting delivers measurable energy savings:

Energy Savings Strategies:

  • Occupancy Sensing: Lights turn off when rooms are unoccupied (15-30% savings)
  • Daylight Harvesting: Dims electric lights when natural light is sufficient (20-40% savings)
  • Dimming: Running LEDs at 50% brightness uses ~40% less energy (not linear)
  • Scheduling: Automatic shutoff at night or when away
  • LED Conversion: LEDs use 75% less energy than incandescent, last 25x longer

Typical Savings: 30-60% reduction in lighting energy costs. For a home spending $150/month on lighting, that's $50-90/month saved, or $600-1,080 annually.

Additional Benefits: Reduced HVAC costs (less heat from lights), longer fixture life, reduced maintenance, utility rebates (available in some areas).
