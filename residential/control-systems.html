The Magic of One-Touch Scenes

Scenes orchestrate multiple systems with a single tap—transforming your home's mood and function instantly.

Good Morning

Your day starts perfectly—automated or manually triggered.

  • • Shades open gradually to natural light
  • • Bedroom lights brighten slowly
  • • Kitchen lights turn on
  • • Thermostat adjusts to daytime temperature
  • • Bathroom radiant floors activate
  • • News or music begins playing

Movie Night

Transform your space into a cinema with one touch.

  • • All lights dim to 5%
  • • Shades close completely
  • • Projector powers on
  • • Audio system activates (surround sound mode)
  • • Screen descends
  • • Climate adjusts for comfort

Entertaining

Set the perfect ambiance for guests.

  • • Living areas set to 60% lighting
  • • Accent lights highlight artwork
  • • Whole-home audio plays party playlist
  • • Outdoor lights illuminate pathways
  • • Pool/spa lights activate
  • • Climate optimized for comfort

Good Night

Secure and prepare your home for sleep.

  • • All lights turn off (except nightlights)
  • • Doors lock automatically
  • • Security system arms to "Stay" mode
  • • Thermostats lower for sleeping
  • • Shades close
  • • Audio systems power down
