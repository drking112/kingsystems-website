Smart Shading for Modern Commercial Spaces

The right shading system does more than block sunlight. It eliminates glare on screens, reduces HVAC load, protects furnishings, and creates a more productive environment for your team and clients. King Systems designs and installs commercial automated shading solutions from the industry's leading manufacturers — matched to your specific space, budget, and integration requirements.

As authorized Lutron dealers with deep expertise across multiple brands, we're manufacturer-agnostic. Our job is to find the right solution for your project, not sell you a brand.
