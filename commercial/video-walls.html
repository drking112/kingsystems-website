Video Wall & LED Display Solutions

From trading floors to operations centers, King Systems designs and installs professional video wall solutions that deliver real-time information at scale. We offer both traditional LCD video walls and cutting-edge LED video wall technology—including curved and flexible LED options—to meet any architectural or operational requirement.

Whether you need ultra-narrow bezel LCD displays for control rooms or seamless LED video walls for high-impact lobbies and entertainment venues, our team delivers turnkey solutions with intelligent processing, control systems, and mission-critical reliability.

Video Wall Technologies

LCD Video Walls

Professional-grade LCD displays with ultra-narrow bezels for seamless multi-screen installations.

  • Ultra-narrow bezels (0.9mm - 3.5mm)
  • 4K and 8K resolution options
  • 24/7 commercial-grade panels
  • Ideal for control rooms and operations centers

LED Video Walls

Direct-view LED technology with zero bezels, unlimited size, and flexible/curved configurations.

  • Completely seamless (no bezels)
  • Fine pixel pitch (0.9mm - 2.5mm)
  • Curved and flexible options available
  • Ideal for lobbies, retail, and entertainment

LED Video Wall Options

Flat LED Walls

Traditional flat installations with seamless imagery and unlimited scalability.

  • • Pixel pitch: 0.9mm - 2.5mm
  • • Indoor/outdoor options
  • • Modular cabinet design
  • • Easy serviceability

Curved LED Walls

Architectural curves and panoramic installations that follow your space.

  • • Custom radius design
  • • Convex or concave curves
  • • Immersive viewing experience
  • • Perfect for lobbies & theaters

Flexible LED Panels

Bendable LED modules for creative shapes, columns, and unique installations.

  • • Wrap around columns
  • • Creative architectural shapes
  • • Lightweight construction
  • • Indoor applications
