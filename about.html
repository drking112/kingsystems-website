About King Systems

Experience-Driven Technology Integration Since 2002

Our Story

Founded in 2002, King Systems is a Colorado-based technology integrator trusted by organizations and homeowners who demand reliability, performance, and long-term value from their systems.

For more than two decades, we've designed, installed, and supported audio/video, lighting, automation, and collaboration systems for projects ranging from high-end mountain residences to complex commercial environments across Colorado and beyond.

Our work spans corporate offices, conference rooms, education, hospitality, and luxury residential properties—each built with the same philosophy: technology should be powerful, intuitive, and dependable every day it's used.

What truly sets King Systems apart is our role as a long-term technology partner, not just an installer. We guide our clients through the full lifecycle of their systems—consultation, design, installation, training, ongoing support, and future upgrades—so their technology continues to perform as needs evolve.

As certified partners with industry leaders including Crestron, Lutron, Control4, Q-SYS, Biamp, and Savant, our team brings deep technical expertise and proven solutions to every project. Just as importantly, we understand the realities of operating technology in Colorado—from challenging environments and infrastructure to the expectations of modern, hybrid workplaces.

At King Systems, our goal is simple: deliver technology that works seamlessly, supports productivity, and stands the test of time.

How We Work

Our Process

From initial consultation through ongoing support, we guide you through every phase with expertise and transparency.

1

Consultation

We listen to your goals, assess your space, and understand your workflow or lifestyle. Site visits, needs analysis, and budget discussion—no pressure, just expert guidance.

2

Design

Custom system design tailored to your needs. Detailed drawings, equipment specifications, integration plans, and transparent pricing. You'll know exactly what you're getting.

3

Installation

Professional installation by certified technicians. Clean cable runs, proper mounting, testing, and integration. We coordinate with your builder or work around your schedule.

4

Support

Comprehensive training, documentation, and ongoing support. 24/7 emergency response, proactive monitoring, software updates, and system optimization as your needs evolve.
