About King Systems

Our Story

Founded in 2002, King Systems has grown from a small Denver-based integrator to Colorado's premier luxury technology specialist. With over 22 years of experience, we've completed more than 1,000 residential and commercial projects across the state.

Our expertise spans from multi-million dollar mountain estates in Aspen and Vail to Fortune 500 corporate headquarters in Denver. We specialize in complex, high-performance systems that exceed expectations and stand the test of time.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of Colorado's unique challenges—from high-altitude networking and extreme temperature environments to snow-rated outdoor equipment and climate-aware automation.
