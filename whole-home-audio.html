Curated Audio Systems

Seamless music throughout your entire estate

Back to Residential Solutions

Enjoy your favorite music in every room of your home—from the kitchen to the patio to the master suite. King Systems designs and installs curated audio systems that deliver exceptional sound quality, intuitive control, and seamless integration with your smart home.

Whether you prefer Crestron for ultimate control and enterprise-grade reliability, or Sonos for simplicity, we create audio solutions tailored to your lifestyle and architecture.

Multi-Room Audio

Independent control for every zone—play different music in each room or sync the entire house for parties and gatherings.

Outdoor Audio

Weather-resistant speakers for patios, decks, and poolside areas. Landscape speakers blend seamlessly with Colorado mountain landscaping.

Streaming Integration

Access Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, Pandora, and more. Voice control via Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri for hands-free operation.

High-Resolution Audio

Support for lossless audio formats, vinyl turntable integration, and audiophile-grade components from McIntosh and Sonus Faber.

Unified Control

Control music, volume, and sources from wall keypads, mobile apps, or voice commands. Integrate seamlessly with Crestron systems for professional-grade control.

Invisible Speakers

In-ceiling and in-wall speakers that disappear into your architecture. Premium acoustic performance without visible speakers or wires.
