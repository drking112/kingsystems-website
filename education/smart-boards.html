Interactive Smart Boards & Digital Whiteboards

Transform classrooms with interactive smart boards that enable collaboration, enhance engagement, and support modern teaching methods. King Systems designs and installs smart board systems for K-12 schools, universities, and training facilities across Colorado.

From single-classroom installations to campus-wide smart board deployments, we deliver turnkey solutions with wireless presentation, multi-touch interaction, annotation tools, and seamless integration with your existing classroom technology.

Smart Board Technologies

Interactive Flat Panels

Large-format touchscreen displays with built-in computing, Android apps, and wireless mirroring.

  • • 65"–98" multi-touch displays
  • • 4K Ultra HD resolution
  • • Built-in speakers & microphones
  • • Annotation and whiteboarding
  • • Screen sharing (AirPlay, Miracast)

Digital Whiteboards

Cloud-connected smart boards with infinite canvas, collaboration tools, and lesson recording.

  • • Infinite canvas & zoom
  • • Multi-user collaboration
  • • Cloud storage & sync
  • • Lesson capture & playback
  • • Student device integration

Interactive Projectors

Ultra-short throw projectors with touch or pen interaction for large surfaces and budget-conscious deployments.

  • • Ultra-short throw projection
  • • Touch or stylus interaction
  • • Up to 100" interactive area
  • • Works on any surface
  • • Cost-effective solution
